Mozilla Sunbird, Portable Edition is the handy Mozilla Sunbird™ calendar and task manager packaged with a PortableApps.com Launcher as a portable app, so you can take your schedule and to do list with you. The Secondary Profile launcher allows you to easily have a second profile for Sunbird Portable and use it with the PortableApps.com Platform. Learn more about Mozilla Sunbird...
